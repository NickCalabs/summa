## One-Paragraph Product Description

Summa is a free, open-source, self-hosted net worth tracker built for people who refuse to pay $249/year to store their financial life on someone else's server. Track every asset class in one place — stocks, crypto, real estate, vehicles, debts, and more — with automatic price syncing, daily snapshots, and a clean spreadsheet UI that puts the data front and center. Your numbers live on your server, in your database, under your control. No subscriptions. No telemetry. No vendor lock-in.

## Product Hunt Description

**Summa — The open-source, self-hosted net worth tracker**

Kubera charges up to $2,499/year to track your net worth. Summa does the same thing — for free, on your own server.

**What it does:**
- Spreadsheet-style UI to track every asset and debt you own
- Auto-synced prices for stocks (Yahoo Finance) and crypto (CoinGecko)
- Full history: daily snapshots + net worth chart over time
- Docker Compose deployment in under 60 seconds
- REST API for everything — the web UI is just one client
- Dark mode, multi-currency, CSV import

**Why it's different:**
- Your data **never leaves your server**. No telemetry, no phone-home, no cloud accounts
- Free forever — AGPL-3.0 licensed
- Built for the same audience as Kubera, at $0/year instead of $249+

If you've ever paid for Kubera, used Maybe Finance, or self-host Ghostfolio and wished it tracked more than just investments — Summa is for you.
